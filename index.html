What is KondeCoin?

Welcome to

KondeCoin

Konde Coin is a community-run technology powering the cryptocurrency Konde (KDC) and thousands of decentralized applications. We are an open source peer to peer digital currency.

Konde coin is an opensource peer-to-peer cryptocurrency that utilises blockchain technology, a highly secure decentralised system of storing information as a public ledger that is maintained by a network of computers called nodes.
